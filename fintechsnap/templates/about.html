FinMentor is a lightweight personal finance mentor that gives you a quick financial health score, simple tips and a 30-day action plan all without bank logins or complicated tracking.

1. Enter a few numbers

Monthly income, expenses, and savings — it takes less than a minute.

2. Get an instant score

A simple rule-based score and a savings rate estimate to show where you stand.

3. Actionable steps

Clear tips and a short 30-day plan you can follow to improve your score. A savings rate estimate shows where you stand.

CORE FEATURES

Privacy first

We never ask for bank credentials. All calculations happen locally or in session.

Fast & simple

Designed for quick classroom demos and real users who want instant clarity.

Actionable tips

Practical suggestions you can apply immediately.

Dashboard

See your score, savings rate and saved tips on a simple dashboard.

Privacy & data

FinMentor is a demo app for your final year project. We do not store personal financial data long-term. On this demo, inputs are saved in session for the dashboard and not shared. If you later add persistence, show a clear opt-in and a privacy policy.

FAQ

Do I need to connect my bank?
No — this is intentionally simple and private. No bank logins required.

Is the score accurate?
It is a rule-based estimate designed to guide users. It can be extended later using refined rules or machine learning.
